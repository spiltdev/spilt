## How to Use This

1. Create the form on Tally (tally.so). It's free and clean.
2. Share the link with anyone who tries the testnet
3. Use conditional logic: show Q7 only if Q2 = "Nostr relay operation"
4. Also send to anyone who engages with your posts and seems interested
5. Review responses weekly and update priorities accordingly
